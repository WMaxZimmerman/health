* Bicep Tendinitis
** Seated Cervical Retraction                              :seated:no_equip:
| Reps | Sets | Hold | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    1 | -    |     3 |      5 |
  
*** Setup
Begin sitting in an upright position with your feet flat on the floor.

*** Movement
Gently draw your chin in, while keeping your eyes fixed on something in front of
you.

*** Tip
Make sure that you do not look down as you do this exercise, or bend your neck
forward.

** Seated Scapular Retraction                              :seated:no_equip:
| Reps | Sets | Hold | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    1 | -    |     3 |      5 |

*** Setup
Begin sitting in an upright position.

*** Movement
Gently squeeze your shoulder blades together, relax, and then repeat.

*** Tip
Make sure to maintain good posture during the exercise.

** Seated Isometric Elbow Flexion                          :seated:no_equip:
| Reps | Sets | Hold  | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+-------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    1 | 5 sec |     2 |      5 |

*** Setup
Begin sitting in an upright position with one elbow bent at 90 degrees, hand in
a fist, and your other hand flat (palm down) on top of your fist.

*** Movement
Try to bend your upward, while resisting with your other hand so that your arm
does not move. Relax, and repeat.

*** Tip
There should be a little to no movement during this exercise.

** Seated Isometric Shoulder Flexion                       :seated:no_equip:
| Reps | Sets | Hold  | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+-------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    1 | 5 sec |     2 |      5 |

*** Setup
Begin sitting upright in a chair.

*** Movement
Lift one arm straight out in front of your body with your thumb pointing up.
Place your other hand on your forearm to prevent further movement of your arm.
Continue to press your raised arm up towards the ceiling and into your hand.
Relax and repeat.

*** Tip
Make sure not to raise your arm above shoulder height and try not to shrug your
shoulders during the exercise. There should be little to no movement.

** Doorway Pec Stretch at 90 Degrees abduction            :standing:doorway:
| Reps | Sets | Hold   | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+--------+-------+--------|
|    3 |    1 | 30 sec |     1 |      5 |

*** Setup
Begin in a standing upright position in the center of a doorway.

*** Movement
With your elbow bent, place your forearms on the sides of the doorway at a 90
degree angle from your sides, then take a small step forward until you feel a
stretch in the front of your shoulders. Hold this position.

*** Tip
Make sure to maintain a gentle stretch and do not shrug your shoulders during
the exercise.

** Scapular Retraction with Resistance                       :standing:band:
| Reps | Sets | Hold | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    3 | -    |     1 |      5 |

*** Setup
Begin standing upright with your elbows bent and tucked at your sides, holding
the ends of a resistance band that is anchored in front of you.

*** Movement
Squeeze your shoulder blades together and downward, pulling against the
resistance band. Hold this position, then relax and repeat.

*** Tip
Make sure to keep your back straight and do not shrug your shoulders during the
exercise.

** Sidelying Shoulder External Rotation w/ Dumbbell         :lying:dumbbell:
| Reps | Sets | Hold | Daily | Weekly |
|------+------+------+-------+--------|
|   10 |    3 | -    |     1 |      3 |

*** Setup
Begin lying on your side with a towel roll under your upper arm and elbow bent
at 90 degrees, holding a dumbbell in your hand. Place a pillow or towel roll
under your head for support.

*** Movement
Rotate your arm upward, then return to the starting position and repeat.

*** Tip
Make sure to keep your elbow bent at 90 degree angle and your arm tucked against
the towel.
